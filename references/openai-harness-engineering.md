## Stable Principles

- Treat the harness as agent infrastructure, not as a large prompt.
- Keep the repository as the system of record for plans, runtime state, artifacts, and durable knowledge.
- Use progressive disclosure: short entry points should point to focused docs.
- Convert important architecture and quality expectations into mechanical checks.
- Make runtime behavior observable through logs, traces, screenshots, evals, and reproducible commands.
- Separate reusable framework rules from product-specific architecture and workflow.
- Plan for increasing autonomy with explicit permission boundaries and escalation points.
